# Magic Travel Website Structure

## Main Pages

1. **Homepage** (/)
   - Hero section with tagline and call-to-action
   - Featured destinations
   - Testimonials
   - Newsletter signup

2. **About** (/about)
   - Yuliya's story and introduction
   - Mission and values
   - Travel philosophy

3. **Destinations** (/destinations)
   - Grid of all destinations
   - Filtering by region/type
   - Individual destination pages

4. **Trips** (/trips)
   - Upcoming group trips
   - Trip details (itinerary, pricing, dates)
   - Booking information

5. **Blog** (/blog)
   - Travel stories and tips
   - Destination guides
   - Personal experiences

6. **Contact** (/contact)
   - Contact form
   - Social media links
   - FAQ section
